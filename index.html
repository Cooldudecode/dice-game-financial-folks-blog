The Money Dice Game 🎲

You start off with $100. Roll the dice 5 times and see what happens to your $100. Will inflation shrink it or will interest grow it?

This game simulates what happens to your money in the real world. Some rolls represent inflation, prices going up, which means your money buys less. Other rolls represent interest, your money earning more over time.

You start with $100. Roll the dice 5 times and track what happens!

Reflect on what happened

1. Rolls 1–3 represented inflation, prices going up so your money buys less. Did inflation hurt you in this game? What does that tell you about leaving money just sitting around in cash?

2. Rolls 4–6 represented interest, your money earning more over time. If you could pick one result to happen to your real money every year, which roll would you want? Why?

3. In real life, you can choose to save and invest rather than keep money in cash. Based on this game, why does it matter WHERE you keep your money, not just how much you have?
